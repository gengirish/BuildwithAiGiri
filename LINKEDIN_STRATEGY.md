### Subject Line A/B Testing

Track open rates on these subject line variants and double down on winners:

**High curiosity:**
- `I'll build your MVP this week — for free`
- `Your idea + 7 days = working product`
- `Can I build something for you? (Seriously, no cost)`

**Social proof:**
- `[X] founders submitted. Spots left for Week [Y].`
- `I just shipped an MVP in 5 days. Yours could be next.`

**Personal:**
- `Loved your post about [topic] — quick idea`
- `[Mutual connection] thought we should connect`

**Urgency:**
- `Week [X] slot closing Friday — got an idea?`
- `3 spots left in the 25-week build challenge`

### Key Rules for Cold Outreach

1. **Personalize the first line.** Reference their specific idea, post, or company. Never send a generic blast.
2. **Keep it under 150 words.** Founders are busy. Respect their time.
3. **One clear CTA.** Either "submit here" or "reply to this email." Not both.
4. **No attachments.** They trigger spam filters and reduce trust.
5. **Follow up exactly once.** If no reply after the follow-up, move on.
6. **Track everything.** Use a simple spreadsheet: Name, Email, Date Sent, Opened, Replied, Submitted.
7. **Send from your personal email** (gen.girish@gmail.com), not a marketing tool. Personal > professional for cold outreach.
8. **Best send times:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9-11 AM in the recipient's timezone.

### How to Use These Frameworks

| Week Stage | Best Framework | Why |
|------------|---------------|-----|
| Pre-launch / idea collection | Contrarian Insight | Challenges beliefs, sparks debate, high shares |
| Mid-week (Day 2-3 of build) | Production Lesson | Shows real work, builds trust through vulnerability |
| After shipping | Market Validation | Positions you as proof of a larger trend |
| Opening a new week slot | Announcement with Credibility | Clear CTA, scarcity, concrete details |
| Post-ship recap | Production Lesson (Build Log) | Radical transparency, strongest social proof |

### Writing Checklist (apply to every post)

- [ ] First 2 lines work as a standalone hook (visible before "...see more")
- [ ] At least one concrete number (years, days, features, %)
- [ ] No more than 3 sentences per paragraph
- [ ] One clear CTA (submit link OR question — not both)
- [ ] No emojis in the body (only sparingly in section markers)
- [ ] Reads well on mobile (short lines, lots of whitespace)
- [ ] Ends with a question OR a forward-looking statement
- [ ] Hashtags at the bottom, not inline
